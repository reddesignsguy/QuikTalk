QuikTalk

QuikTalk is a simple-to-use Video Call application. To connect with another user, all you need to do is follow these steps!

1. First, click the "Start Webcam" Button, to begin (Your video will appear under the title "My Webcam!")

2. If you want to start a conversation, Create a new Call (instructions...)

3. If you want to join a conversation, Join a Call (instructions...)

Answer the call from a different browser window or device
